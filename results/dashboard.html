Key Takeaways: ① Louvain + SAGE-CAAN achieves the highest node accuracy (69.5%) on WikiCS, outperforming all baselines including DistDGL (66.6%). ② CAAN aggregation consistently boosts accuracy by 15–30 percentage points over raw community models. ③ Louvain partitioning significantly outperforms LPA for node classification, with faster execution due to fewer, larger communities. ④ Link AUC remains at 0.50 for CAAN models — expected since CAAN focuses on node classification via cross-community aggregation.
